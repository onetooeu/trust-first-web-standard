# Roadmap (Public)
This roadmap expresses intent, not guarantees.

## Near-term
- Expand OpenAPI coverage
- Improve transparency feeds
- Add certification workflow pages and forms

## Mid-term
- White-label trust endpoints (per-client signing)
- Streaming updates (webhooks + feeds)
- Governance reporting automation

## Long-term
- EU-wide trust registry scaling
- Interoperability with emerging AI governance standards
