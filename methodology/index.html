Methodology Snapshot

ONETOO.eu (operated by ONETOO.dynamics) publishes a high-level methodology overview to support transparency without exposing sensitive implementation details.

  • Signals: Multiple trust and risk signals; no single signal decides alone.
  • Time: We consider trends and stability over time, not only snapshots.
  • Jurisdiction: EU core + national overrides.
  • Explainability: Outputs include reasons and confidence.
  • Auditability: Policies and key datasets are versioned and integrity-protected.
