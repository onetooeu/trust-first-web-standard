Privacy

ONETOO.eu (operated by ONETOO.dynamics) is built to minimize personal data processing.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not require accounts for public endpoints.
  • We do not use invasive tracking for verified redirects.

Operational data

For reliability and abuse prevention, we may process limited technical logs (e.g., timestamps, request volume, aggregated metrics). We aim to keep logs minimal and privacy-preserving.

Affiliate transparency

Where affiliate links are used, they are clearly labeled. Trust comes before monetization.
