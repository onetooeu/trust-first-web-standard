# Enterprise Demo Scenarios (1‑page)

## Demo 1 — AI assistant for a customer
**Situation:** AI recommends a merchant.
**Problem:** Unverified store may cause harm.
**How ONETOO.eu helps:** Returns *safe/neutral/risky*, **confidence**, reason codes, and jurisdiction context.
**Outcome:** AI gives an explainable recommendation and discloses affiliate relationships.

## Demo 2 — Large comparison platform
**Situation:** Platform links to many merchants.
**Problem:** Reputational risk and user complaints.
**How ONETOO.eu helps:** Trust overlay + **verified traffic** via safe redirects.
**Outcome:** Higher-quality outbound traffic and fewer high-risk incidents.

## Demo 3 — EU marketplace (multi-country)
**Situation:** Operates across many EU countries.
**Problem:** Different local expectations and compliance patterns.
**How ONETOO.eu helps:** **EU core + national overrides** and cross-border risk diff.
**Outcome:** One API with jurisdiction-aware decisions.

## Demo 4 — Audit / compliance review
**Situation:** Need to understand why decisions were made.
**How ONETOO.eu helps:** Transparency reports, incident pages, versioned policies, and auditable logs.
**Outcome:** Clear, reviewable decision trail.
