# ADR-0003: Explainability and confidence required in outputs
Date: 2025-12-19
Status: Accepted

## Context
Trust outputs must be usable by humans and AI, and withstand audits.

## Decision
Every risk evaluation MUST include reason codes and confidence. Trend (temporal memory) is preferred when available.

## Consequences
- Better human understanding
- Safer AI decisions
- More reliable compliance posture
